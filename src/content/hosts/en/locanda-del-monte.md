## Our Accommodation

Locanda del Monte is a family-run establishment located in the historic center of Amandola, the starting city for the Via dei Sette Santi. With a hospitality tradition passed down through three generations, we offer pilgrims and visitors an authentic experience in the heart of the Sibillini Mountains.

### Services for Pilgrims

We understand the special needs of pilgrims and offer:

- **Early breakfast** for morning departures
- **Packed lunch** for the day's walking
- **Laundry service** for technical clothing
- **Equipment storage** secure and free
- **Trail information** and weather conditions
- **Booking assistance** for subsequent stages

### Traditional Restaurant

Our restaurant offers the best Marche cuisine, with typical dishes prepared with local and organic ingredients:

- Marchigiani vincisgrassi
- Grilled meat
- Local truffle specialties
- Regional cheeses and cured meats
- Selected Marche wines

### Our Rooms

All rooms feature:
- Private bathroom with shower
- Satellite TV
- Free WiFi
- Air conditioning
- Panoramic views of the Sibillini
