## Our Refuge

Rifugio Monte Sibilla is located in a panoramic position at 1400 meters altitude, immersed in the heart of the Sibillini Mountains National Park. An ideal place for those walking the Via dei Sette Santi and for all mountain enthusiasts.

### For Hikers

We offer services designed for walkers:

- **Flexible check-in** to adapt to walking times
- **Abundant packed lunch** with local products
- **Free drying** of shoes and clothing
- **Charging point** for electronic devices
- **Maps and information** about local trails
- **Transfer service** from/to Montefortino

### Mountain Cuisine

Our kitchen offers traditional mountain dishes with zero-kilometer products:

- Hot soups and broths
- Polenta with wild boar or sausage
- Artisan cheeses and cured meats
- Homemade desserts
- Local wine

### Rooms and Services

We offer different types of accommodation:
- Double rooms with private bathroom
- Dormitories for 4-6 people
- Shared bathrooms on floors
- Hot water always available
- Blankets and sheets included

## Strategic Position

The refuge is located:
- ✅ 3 km from Montefortino
- ✅ Near the Church of San Leonardo (stage 1)
- ✅ On the trail connecting several stages
- ✅ In a panoramic area overlooking the Sibillini
- ✅ Surrounded by woods and meadows
