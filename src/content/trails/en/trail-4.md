# From Madonna dell'Ambro Sanctuary to Amandola

The fourth stage leads from the sanctuary to the medieval town of Amandola, gradually descending from the mountain to the valley.

## Route Description

Leaving the sanctuary, the path descends through woods and meadows, continuously offering new perspectives on the valley below. You cross once-cultivated terraces, testimony to the ancient mountain farming civilization.

As you approach Amandola, the landscape opens to panoramic views of the medieval town, with its towers and bell towers standing out against the sky.

## Points of Interest

- **Rural landscape**: terraces and old farmhouses
- **Amandola**: fortified medieval town
- **Historic churches**: Santa Maria di Marte and San Giovanni ai Ponti
- **Historic center**: squares, arcades, and traditional architecture
