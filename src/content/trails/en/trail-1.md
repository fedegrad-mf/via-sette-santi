# From San Ruffino to Rubbiano

The first stage of the Via dei Sette Santi represents a perfect introduction to this spiritual journey. Starting from the evocative Church of San Ruffino, located on a promontory overlooking the valley, the path winds through gentle and relaxing landscapes.

## Route Description

The route begins with a short climb that allows you to enjoy a panoramic view of the Tenna Valley. Subsequently, the path crosses ancient oak forests, offering shade and coolness during summer months.

Along the way, you'll encounter several medieval villages that preserve their ancient charm intact. You can stop to admire the traditional architecture and taste local products.

## Points of Interest

- **San Ruffino Church**: starting point of the trail, Romanesque building from the 12th century
- **Panoramic views**: breathtaking glimpses of the Sibillini Mountains chain
- **Rubbiano**: medieval village, arrival point of the first stage
